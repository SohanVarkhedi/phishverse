// THE PLATFORM

Adaptive training built on real attacker behaviour.

PHISHVERSE simulates the entire attack lifecycle inside a controlled environment, then trains each employee with AI-curated lessons calibrated to the biases they personally fell for.

01 / 05OVERVIEW
AI COPILOT

A model that profiles every click, hover and hesitation.

The PHISHVERSE engine analyses behavioural telemetry from each simulated attack to build a psychological risk profile per employee — then tailors lectures, drills and certifications to seal the exact gap.

  • Per-user bias profile across urgency, authority, reward, and fear vectors.
  • Adaptive MCQ exams that escalate difficulty until mastery is proven.
  • Generative campaign authoring with industry-specific context grounding.
  • Manager debriefs auto-written for board, audit, and ISO 27001 reporting.
CINEMATIC TRAINING

A subtle RPG layer that turns awareness into mastery.

Employees progress through semester-style cycles — Entrance Exam → Campaign → Lectures → Report → Final Exam → Certificate — earning verifiable credentials without the cringe of gamification.

  • Story-driven simulations grounded in real corporate incidents.
  • Failure becomes the lesson — every misstep is decoded by the AI debrief.
  • Certificates signed and verifiable on internal SSO and HRIS pipelines.
